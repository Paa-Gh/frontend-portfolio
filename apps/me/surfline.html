Who We Are


Surfline Communications Limited was established in 2011 to provide premium quality wireless broadband connectivity to the Ghanaian market.


Surfline launched to immediately become the first 4G LTE company in Ghana and leading single largest LTE ever deployed in Sub-Saharan Africa with world class strategic partners such as IBM, Alcatel Lucent, Microsoft and Huawei.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to be a national leader in the provision of connectivity solutions that accelerates Ghana's digital development agenda.


VISION: A Ghana where people and businesses experience true mobile broadband and the possibilities it brings.
